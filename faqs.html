FAQs

Q1: What is the main function of the Home (Political) Department?

The Home (Political) Department primarily deals with issues relating to Law & Order in the State of Meghalaya and other political matters as assigned by the Government.

Q2: How can I contact the Home (Political) Department?

You can visit the Contact Us page for all contact details and communication channels.

Q3: Where can I find official notifications and reports?

Official notifications and reports are available in the Documents section of the sidebar and on the main website under the Reports section.

Q4: Who is the head of the Home (Political) Department?

The Chief Secretary, Government of Meghalaya, heads the department, assisted by other senior officials.

Q5: How do I access services for differently abled persons?

Accessibility features such as screen reader access, text size adjustment, and contrast modes are available via the utility bar at the top of the page.
