Ensuring Safety and Governance in Meghalaya

About the Department

The Home (Political) Department is headed by the Chief Secretary, Government of Meghalaya, assisted by the Additional Chief Secretary, Principal Secretary, Commissioner & Secretary, Joint Secretary, Deputy Secretary, and Under Secretary. The Chief Minister holds the direct charge of the Political Department.

The Department primarily addresses matters concerning law and order in the state and does not have any subordinate offices under its control.

Key Subjects Handled by the Department

  1. Assam-Meghalaya boundary dispute.
  2. Preventive Detention under National Security Act, 1980 and Meghalaya Preventive Detention Act, 1995 (MPDA); submission of detention and release reports under NSA, 1980.
  3. Compilation of detention list under Union War Book and matters related to Internal Security.
  4. Disturbed Area Armed Forces (Special Powers) Act, 1958.
  5. Cipher-related matters.
  6. National Foundation for Communal Harmony.
  7. Indira Gandhi Award for National Integration.
  8. Observation of Integration Week (Quami Ekta Week) and Anti-Terrorism Day.
  9. Sadbhabana Diwas.
  10. National Integration Council matters.
  11. Inter-State Council matters.
  12. Sarkaria Commission matters.
  13. Visits of President, Vice President, Prime Minister, Foreign Diplomats, Governors, and Chief Ministers of other states.
  14. Construction and coordination of Indo-Bangladesh border fencing.
  15. State Level Public Grievances Committee.
  16. Implementation of Official Language policy.
  17. Essential Services Maintenance Act, 1980/1981.
  18. Negotiations with militant groups.
  19. Rehabilitation of surrendered and disbanded cadres.
  20. Handling complaints of corruption, vigilance cases, and prosecution sanctions referred to the Political Department.
  21. Issuance of Vigilance Clearance Certificates.
  22. Law and order situation in the State.
  23. Ex-gratia and compensation for:
    • Victims of communal riots.
    • Next of kin of security personnel killed during counter-insurgency or terrorism actions.
  24. Matters related to Lokayukta.
  25. State and National Human Rights Commission (NHRC) matters.
  26. Victim Compensation Scheme.
  27. Benami Transaction Act and associated issues.
  28. Security arrangements for Central Ministers, VIPs, and Secretariat Buildings.
  29. Prevention and response to communal riots.
  30. Medals and awards for Distinguished/Meritorious/Gallantry services.
  31. Preparation of Independence Day and Republic Day speeches.
  32. Authentication of documents for foreign visits.
  33. Appointment and swearing-in of the Governor of Meghalaya.
