About the Department

The Home (Political) Department is headed by the Chief Secretary, Government of Meghalaya assisted by the Additional Chief Secretary, Principal Secretary, Commissioner & Secretary, Joint Secretary, Deputy Secretary and Under Secretary. The Chief Minister holds the direct charge of Political Department.

The Department deals mainly with the issues relating to Law & Order in the State. The Department does not have any subordinate offices under its control.
